Planner Insight

With the current setup, 108 concurrent seats are available for productive usage. Under a 24×7 and 5-working-day model, the current floor supports about 274 roster HC, but only around 192 productive HC after shrinkage. Since support seats are fixed, 18 seats are directly locked for support staff and cannot be reused like agent seats. Under the dummy scenario, the total seat need is estimated at 144, which means the business is short by 24 seats against the recommended requirement.

Formula Glossary

Concurrent Seat Capacity = Total Seats - Dedicated / Blocked Seats

Agent Seats Required = Peak Agent Seat Load adjusted by HOOP and shift mix

Support Seats Required = Support HC if support seats are fixed, otherwise calculated with shared-seat logic

Total Seats Required = Agent Seats Required + Support Seats Required

Roster HC Supported = Concurrent Seats × Effective Reuse Factor × (HOOP Days ÷ Working Days per Agent)

Productive HC Supported = Roster HC Supported × (1 - Shrinkage)
