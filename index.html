Seat Requirement & Utilization Planner

Mode-based seat planning tool for HC capacity from seats, seats needed from HC, seat utilization review, and shift mix optimization. This version uses live calculations instead of dummy-only display logic.

Planning Inputs

HC Capacity from Seats:
Calculates how much roster HC and productive HC your current seat base can support.
Seats Needed from HC:
Calculates the seat base required to support the target HC after shrinkage and seat reuse assumptions.
Seat Utilization Planner:
Reviews current seat usage, waste hours, and utilization pressure on the floor.
Shift Mix Optimization:
Compares standard, high-utilization, and max-utilization shift wave designs and recommends the best fit.

Formula Glossary

Usable Production Seats

Total Seats Available − Blocked Seats − Fixed Support Seats
Effective Seat Block Hours

Average Shift Length + (Handover Gap Minutes ÷ 60)
Theoretical Reuse Factor

HOOP Hours ÷ Effective Seat Block Hours
Practical Reuse Factor

Theoretical Reuse Factor × Reuse Efficiency Factor
Roster HC Supported

Usable Production Seats × Practical Reuse Factor × (HOOP Days ÷ Working Days per Agent)
Productive HC Supported

Roster HC Supported × (1 − Shrinkage %)
Seats Needed from HC

Required Roster HC ÷ (Practical Reuse Factor × (HOOP Days ÷ Working Days per Agent))
Required Roster HC

Required Productive HC ÷ (1 − Shrinkage %), then apply planning buffer
