Formula Glossary

All key logic used in this page, placed at the end for easier reference.

Usable Seats ?

Physical production seats available after blocked and support seats are removed.

Usable Seats = Total Seats − Blocked Seats − Support Seats

Base Weekly Roster HC ?

Clean weekly roster support before any seat reuse adjustment is applied.

Base Weekly Roster HC = Seat Base Used × (HOOP Days ÷ Working Days per Agent)

Theoretical Seat Turns ?

How many seat turns are possible per day before optimization losses are applied.

Theoretical Seat Turns = HOOP Hours ÷ (Shift Length + Handover Hours)

Seat Reuse Multiplier ?

Practical seat-turn factor after applying optimization style efficiency.

Seat Reuse Multiplier = Theoretical Seat Turns, if Theoretical ≤ 1.0 Seat Reuse Multiplier = Theoretical Seat Turns × Optimization Efficiency, if Theoretical > 1.0

Adjusted Weekly Roster HC ?

Weekly roster support after seat reuse is applied.

Adjusted Weekly Roster HC = Base Weekly Roster HC × Seat Reuse Multiplier × (1 − Capacity Buffer %)

Weekly Productive HC Capacity ?

Final supportable productive HC after shrinkage is applied.

Weekly Productive HC Capacity = Adjusted Weekly Roster HC × (1 − Shrinkage %)

Required Roster HC ?

Used only in Seats Needed from HC mode to convert productive demand back into roster demand.

Required Roster HC = Required Productive HC ÷ (1 − Shrinkage %)

Production Seats Needed ?

Production seats required to support the target HC under the selected assumptions.

Production Seats Needed = Required Roster HC ÷ [(HOOP Days ÷ Working Days) × Seat Reuse Multiplier]

Shift Hours / Day ?

Full occupied shift hours used for seat-hour generation in the scenario comparison.

Generated Seat Hours / Day = Σ (Wave Seats × Shift Length)

Generated Seat Hours / Day ?

Total seat-hours created by all active waves in a shift mix scenario.

Generated Seat Hours / Day = Σ (Wave Seats × Shift Length)

Overlap Waste / Day ?

Seat-hours lost because adjacent waves overlap and not all overlap creates useful extra coverage.

Overlap Waste / Day = Σ [Min(Adjacent Wave Seats) × Overlap Hours × Overlap Waste Factor]

Net Useful Seat Hours / Day ?

Actual useful seat-hours left after subtracting overlap waste.

Net Useful Seat Hours / Day = Generated Seat Hours / Day − Overlap Waste / Day

Coverage Efficiency ?

How much of generated seat-hours remains useful after overlap loss.

Coverage Efficiency % = Net Useful Seat Hours / Day ÷ Generated Seat Hours / Day × 100

Seat Utilization ?

Useful occupied seat-hours compared with total available seat-hours in the operating window.

Seat Utilization % = Net Useful Seat Hours / Day ÷ (Seat Base × HOOP Hours) × 100

Idle Seat Hours / Week ?

Generated seat-hours that are not needed by the demand load.

Idle Seat Hours / Week = Max(0, Generated Seat Hours / Day − Daily Demand Hours) × HOOP Days

Productive HC Supported ?

How many HC can be supported by the useful seat-hours from that scenario.

Productive HC Supported = Weekly Useful Seat Hours ÷ (Shift Length × Working Days)

Uncovered Demand Hours ?

Remaining demand not covered by the useful seat-hours of the shift mix.

Uncovered Demand Hours = Max(0, Weekly Demand Hours − Weekly Useful Seat Hours)
