Planner Insight

In this redesigned dummy view, the page is mapped more logically for HC Capacity from Seats. Since the main question is how much production HC can be supported from a fixed seat base, the inputs focus on seats, HOOP, working days, shift length, shrinkage, and fixed support seats rather than broader HC requirement fields. This makes the page more relevant for Operations and leadership discussions where the user wants a seat-based capacity projection first.

Formula Glossary

Usable Production Seats

Total Seats Available − Blocked Seats − Fixed Support Seats
Seat Reuse Factor

HOOP Hours ÷ Average Shift Length, adjusted for overlap and handover tolerance
Roster HC Supported

Usable Production Seats × Seat Reuse Factor × (HOOP Days ÷ Working Days per Agent)
Productive HC Supported

Roster HC Supported × (1 − Shrinkage %)
